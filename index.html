About Sharks

Sharks have been around for over 400 million years, making them one of the oldest creatures on Earth. They play a crucial role in maintaining the health of marine ecosystems.

Shark Facts

  • There are over 500 species of sharks.
  • Sharks never stop swimming because they need to keep water flowing over their gills to breathe.
  • Some sharks can go into freshwater rivers, like Bull sharks!

Popular Shark Species

Bull Shark

Known for its aggression and ability to survive in both salt and freshwater, the Bull shark is one of the most unique shark species.

Tiger Shark

With its iconic stripes and a diverse diet, the Tiger shark is one of the ocean's most adaptable predators.
